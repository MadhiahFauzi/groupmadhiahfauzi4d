Company Background


Established in 2015 by EN MOHD RAFIQ BIN HASSAN

Kico Baby Centre (KBC) also known as Kidz Collection Corner. Before he have the store, in 2015, he running the business just from home. This company started running their business by selling kekabu mattres for the first time. This company used online, offline and shop as a medium of their business. For online business, it has about 80 dropship from different state such as Kuala Lumpur, Perak, Negeri Sembilan and Johore.

Our company started running this business by selling kekabu mattres and begining 2016 it start import baby product from china with 36 suppliers. The high achievement of this company is we earn a sales more than RM2.6 million for a year. Most of the sales is from the online business. It is about 80%. For now, this company has 2 outlet of shop which are the main shop is at Tendong, Pasir Mas and the second outlet is at Kubang Kerian. The second outlet started operated on 1 Jan 2020.
